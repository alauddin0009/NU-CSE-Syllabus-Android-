
<h2><strong>GED-316 Technical Writing &amp; Communications</strong></h2>
<p>3 hours in a week, 3.00 Cr.</p>
<p><strong>&nbsp;</strong></p>
<p><strong>Communication in the workplace:</strong> Definition, Scope, role, principles, function, and objective.</p>
<p><strong>The processes of human communication:</strong> Definition, steps, feedback.</p>
<p><strong>Adaptation and the selection of words:</strong> the basics need for adaptation, suggestion for selecting words, suggestion for nondiscriminatory writing.</p>
<p><strong>Construction of clear sentences and paragraph:</strong> The basic need for adaptation, suggestion for selecting words, suggestion for nondiscriminatory writing.</p>
<p><strong>Writing for effect:</strong> Business etiquette and the need for effect conversational style, you-viewpoint, accent on positive language courtesy, the role of emphasis, coherence.</p>
<p><strong>Directness in Good news:</strong> the process of writing, Routine Inquiries.</p>
<p><strong>Indirectness in Bad-News Message:</strong> Situations report indirectness, Refused request, Adjustment refusal, other indirect messages.</p>
<p>Strategies in the job search process: the job search, Preparing the application documents, Construction the traditional resume, Constructing the electronic resume, Writing the application letter, Handling the interview.</p>
<p><strong>Modern devices in communication/ electronic communication/ Technology in modern communication:</strong> meaning, media, advantage, disadvantage, Internet, e-mail, teleconferencing, multimedia.</p>
<p><strong>Basics of Report writing: </strong>Defining reports, determining the report purpose, determining the factors, gathering the information needed, interpreting the findings, Organizing the repot information, writing the report, collaborative report writing.</p>
<p><strong>Long, Formal reports:</strong> Organizing and content of the longer report, the prefatory parts, the report proper, structural coherence helper.</p>
